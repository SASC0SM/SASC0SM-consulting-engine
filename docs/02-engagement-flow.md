# Engagement Flow — Inbound → Diagnostic → BAF Program

This document defines the end-to-end engagement sequence.

---

## Step 0 — Inbound Capture

**Channels**
- LinkedIn DMs
- Email outreach
- Website form submissions
- Referrals / introductions

**Goal**
Convert all inbound into a single “paid diagnostic” pathway.

---

## Step 1 — Auto-Reply / First Response

**Core action**
Offer a short paid call that provides immediate value.

**Call**
Digital Sovereignty Diagnostic (DSD) — 20–30 mins

**Payment**
BTC (Lightning preferred; on-chain supported)

**Output promise**
- 3 actionable recommendations
- Bitcoin maturity positioning
- A readiness scorecard

---

## Step 2 — Qualification & Scoring

Input: intake form  
Output: lead score + recommended program path

Scoring dimensions:
- Authority (decision maker / influencer / researcher)
- Budget capacity
- Urgency
- BTC maturity
- Infra complexity
- Energy-finance opportunity

---

## Step 3 — Delivery: The Diagnostic Call

Agenda:
1) Current situation (1–2 mins)
2) Risks & constraints (5 mins)
3) Bitcoin adoption opportunities (10 mins)
4) Next-step recommendation (5 mins)

Deliverables:
- call notes
- scorecard PDF
- recommended next tier

---

## Step 4 — Upsell Paths (BAF-Aligned)

BAF-1 Insight → Tier 1 Diagnostic  
BAF-2 Experiment → Tier 2 Readiness  
BAF-3 Commitment → Tier 3 Pilot  
BAF-4 Transformation → Tier 4 Program  
BAF-5 Autonomy → Sovereign OS / Ecosystem

---

## Step 5 — Retention & Expansion

Mechanisms:
- monthly advisory retainer
- quarterly strategy reviews
- roadmap refresh
- continuous pilot iteration
- ecosystem partnerships

---

## Where It Plugs In (SASCOSM Ecosystem)

- SASCOSM Digital Intelligence: advisory + architecture
- SovereignX: payment rails + custody + settlement logic
- MYRA OS: UX, identity, sovereign app store components
- Energy programs: mining, clean power, infra design

---

> The flow is designed to turn low-trust inbound into high-trust sovereign engagements.
